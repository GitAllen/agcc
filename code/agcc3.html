What is Global Customer Playbook for Azure China

The Global Customer Playbook is a step by step guide as well as troubleshooting wizard to help Business decision makers/IT admins/Solution Architects to fully comprehend the proper procedures to setup services in Azure China as well issues that may come up during the process and how to resolve them with minimum efforts. Regulatory considerations, Azure China technology platform, Azure China partner solution offerings, application and service migration guidance such as design patterns samples and scenario based tips are some of the topics covered in depth.
